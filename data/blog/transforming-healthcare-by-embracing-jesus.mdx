## Objectives: Aligning Vision with Action

The primary goal was straightforward yet ambitious: "Place Jesus at the center of healthcare." This meant more than just a slogan; it will involve a comprehensive strategy to attract and engage staff, patients, and the community who shared this vision. The CEO outlined the broad strokes of this bold initiative during our initial discussion, sparking my commitment to a cause that promised significant community impact.
